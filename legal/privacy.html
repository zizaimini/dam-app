Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

1. Overview

Dam is designed with privacy first. The App runs entirely on your device and does not collect or transmit personal data to our servers.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

Dam does not collect, store, or transmit: your screen content, application names, usage patterns, or any personally identifiable information.

3. Purchase Data

When you purchase Dam, payment is processed by Stripe, Inc. We receive only basic transaction information (email address for license delivery, order ID, transaction status). We do not store your payment card details. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data: stripe.com/privacy.

4. Automatic Updates

Dam uses Sparkle to check for updates. Update checks contact our update server (dam.astrocean.love) and include your macOS version and Dam version. No personal data is sent.
